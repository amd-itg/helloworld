# 🏢 About PSSLAI

**Public Safety Savings and Loan Association, Inc. (PSSLAI)** is one of the Philippines' leading savings and loan associations dedicated to serving members of the Public Safety Sector.

### Mission

To provide reliable, innovative, and member-focused financial services that empower those who serve and protect the nation.

### Who Can Become Members?

- 👮 Philippine National Police (PNP)
- 🚒 Bureau of Fire Protection (BFP)
- 👨‍👩‍👧 Eligible Associate Members
- 🏛 Other qualified public safety personnel

### Services

- 💰 Savings Products
- 💳 Salary Loans
- 🏦 Pension Loans
- 📱 Digital Banking Services
- 💸 Bills Payment
- 📲 Mobile App
- 📈 Financial Solutions
